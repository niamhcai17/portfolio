About me

I started my career in project management — coordinating timelines, aligning teams, and making sure things shipped on time. But I kept gravitating toward the design side: wireframes, user flows, how things looked and felt.

So I made the leap. I taught myself to design and code, and now I build the things I envision — from concept to deployed product. Every project here was designed and developed by me.

What I bring is a rare mix: the structured thinking of a PM, the eye of a designer, and the hands of a developer. I don't just hand off mockups — I ship real, working websites.
